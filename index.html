Test cricket record

    1.Dhoni is the first Indian wicket-keeper to score 4,000 Test runs.[187][188]
    2.After hitting a six in the third Test against England in Southampton, Dhoni completed 50 sixes as a captain, an Indian record.[189]
    3.Dhoni, with 294 dismissals in his career, ranks first in the all-time dismissals list by Indian wicket-keepers.[190]

ODI cricket record

    1.Dhoni is the third captain (and the first non-Australian) overall to win 100 games.[191]
    2.First player to pass 10,000 runs in ODI cricket with having a career average of over 50.[192]
    3.Most not outs (84) in ODIs.[193]
    4.Dhoni's 183* against Sri Lanka in 2005 is the highest score by a wicket-keeper.[194]
    5.Dhoni and Bhuvneshwar Kumar were involved in a partnership of 100 not out against Sri Lanka, which is India's highest eighth wicket partnership in ODIs.[195]
    6.Dhoni holds the records of the most dismissals in an innings (6) and career (432) by an Indian wicket-keeper.[196]
    7.Dhoni has the most stumpings (123) by any wicket-keeper in an ODI career, and is so far the only keeper to pass 100 stumpings.[197][198]

T20I cricket record

    1.Dhoni holds the record for playing the most T20I innings (76) and scored the most runs (1,153) before scoring a fifty[199][200][201]
    2.Most stumpings as wicket-keeper in T20Is (34)[202]
    3.Most catches as wicket keeper in a T20I innings (5)[203]

Combined Test, ODI and T20I records

    1.He has played the most international matches as captain (332)[204]
    2.Dhoni is the first, and so far only, wicket-keeper to make 150 stumpings across the three forms of the game. His current total of stumpings in internationals stands at 195[205][206]

National honours

Sporting honours

  • Castrol Indian Cricketer of the Year: 2011[230][231]

  • ICC Men's T20I team of the decade: 2011–2020 (captain and wicketkeeper)[232]
